The Challenge

The one-on-one model with LLM/Agent does not conform to the team work model of human society, which severely limits the development of AI capabilities.

Growing Gaps

One-on-one AI interactions mean everyone's results depend solely on their individual skills, widening the gap between team members and reducing overall effectiveness.

Lost Collective Power

Team knowledge and experience lack a centralized hub, leading to inefficient workflows, scattered information management, and missed opportunities for collaboration.

Vanishing Insights

Brilliant brainstorming sessions and productive conversations disappear the moment meetings end, with no systematic way to capture and build upon valuable insights.

Wasted Traffic

Why let website visitors leave after a single interaction? The challenge is transforming one-time chats into valuable, self-growing communities that provide lasting engagement.
